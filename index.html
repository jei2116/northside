Our Story

A Brooklyn School Built
for Every Student.

Northside Charter High School was founded with a single belief: every child in Brooklyn deserves a high school that sees their potential and actively works to unlock it.

  • Free and open to all Brooklyn students through the NYC school lottery
  • Grades 9–12 with a college preparatory curriculum
  • Personalized academic support and college counseling
  • Athletics, clubs, and student leadership opportunities
  • Located at 424 Leonard St, Williamsburg, Brooklyn

Admissions

How to Apply to
Northside Charter HS.

Applying is free and simple. Northside is a public charter school — open to all Brooklyn students through the NYC school lottery. No test required.

1

Complete the Online Application

Fill out the free application through SchoolMint. It takes about 10 minutes.

2

Attend an Open House or School Tour

Come see the school in action. Meet teachers, students, and the principal.

3

Submit Required Documents

We'll walk you through the paperwork — transcripts, immunization records, and ID.

4

Confirm Your Seat

Once accepted, confirm enrollment and join the Northside Knights family.

Open House — May 2026

Join us every Saturday in May from 10 AM – 11 AM. Guided tours, Q&A with staff, and student presentations. Seats are limited — register to save your spot.
