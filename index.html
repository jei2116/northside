Why Northside?

Everything Your Child Needs
to Thrive in High School and Beyond.

We are a small, close-knit school where every student is known by name and supported to reach their full potential.

Safe and Structured Environment

A calm, orderly campus where students feel secure and respected every day. A stable environment is the foundation for learning.

Rigorous Academic Program

A comprehensive liberal arts curriculum aligned with state standards that prepares students for college-level work and real-world thinking.

Guaranteed College Acceptance

Our college preparatory program ensures every graduating student has a college acceptance in hand. College attainment is not a goal here, it is the expectation.

Small Class Sizes

With intentionally small classes, teachers give every student the individual attention they deserve. No student falls through the cracks at Northside.

Healthcare and Career Pathways

Students gain real-world experience through our Healthcare Pathway and Internship Program, building skills that matter beyond the classroom.

A Community-Rooted Team

Over 35% of our veteran staff have worked at Northside for more than five years. They chose this school and they are invested in every student's success.

Our School

Built for Brooklyn.
Built for Every Student.

Northside Charter High School was founded with one belief: every child in Brooklyn deserves a high school that sees their potential and actively works to unlock it.

  • Free and open to all Brooklyn students through the NYC school lottery
  • Grades 9-12 with a college preparatory curriculum
  • Personalized academic support and college counseling
  • Healthcare Pathway and Internship Program for career readiness
  • Athletics, clubs, and student leadership opportunities
  • Located at 424 Leonard Street, Williamsburg, Brooklyn

Admissions

How to Apply to
Northside Charter HS.

Applying is free and simple. Northside is a public charter school open to all Brooklyn students. No test required. We are currently accepting students for the 2026-2027 school year.

1

Complete the Online Application

Fill out the free application through SchoolMint. It takes about 10 minutes and requires no test scores.

2

Attend an Open House or Tour

Come see the school in action. Meet teachers, students, and our leadership team in person.

3

Submit Required Documents

Our team will guide you through transcripts, immunization records, and ID. We assist every family through the process.

4

Confirm Your Seat

Once accepted, confirm enrollment and join the Northside Knights family. School year 2026-2027 begins September 1, 2026.

Open House - May 2026

Join us every Saturday in May from 10 AM to 11 AM. Guided tours, Q&A with staff, and student presentations. Seats are limited. Register to save your spot.
