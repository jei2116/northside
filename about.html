About Northside

Home of the Knights.
Heart of Brooklyn.

We are a free public charter high school committed to providing every student in Brooklyn with a rigorous, supportive path to college and beyond.

Our Mission

Northside Charter High School's mission is to provide an enriching and innovative learning environment whereby students achieve postsecondary readiness.

Our Vision

To be a college preparatory organization for all students where post-secondary education attainment is the expectation and reality.

The Northside Journey

Get Ready. Go to College.
Complete Your Degree.

The journey we envision for every student is to get ready for, go to, and complete the right-fit college or university of their choice. That vision shapes everything we do.

  • Comprehensive, rigorous liberal arts program aligned with state standards
  • Access to modern technologies and current teaching methods
  • Data-driven instruction that continuously improves outcomes
  • One-on-one college counseling for every student
  • Real-world internship and career pathway programs
  • A guaranteed college acceptance for every graduate

Our Students

The Northside Knight.

Hardworking

Our students come to school ready to put in the effort. They embrace challenge and take pride in the results of their commitment.

Kind-Hearted

Northside students lead with empathy. They support their peers, lift up their community, and bring a genuine spirit of care to everything they do.

Adaptive

The world changes fast. Our students learn to think critically, respond to new challenges, and grow from every experience inside and outside the classroom.

Academic Programs

Beyond the Classroom.

Northside offers specialized programs that connect learning to real careers and real opportunities for every student.

01

Healthcare Pathway

Students interested in health careers gain foundational knowledge, certifications, and exposure to healthcare settings through Northside's dedicated pathway program.

02

Internship Program

Northside partners with organizations across Brooklyn and New York City to offer students real internship experiences before graduation.

03

College Counseling

Every student works directly with our college counselor to identify the right-fit schools, complete applications, and secure financial aid. College acceptance is guaranteed.

04

Liberal Arts Core

A comprehensive program spanning mathematics, English, science, social studies, arts, and physical education. Rigorous, standards-aligned, and built for college readiness.

05

Athletics

The Northside Knights compete in team sports and individual events including track and field. Our athletic programs build discipline, teamwork, and school pride.

06

Student Life and Leadership

Clubs, events, student government, and community engagement opportunities help Northside students develop as leaders and contributing members of their community.
